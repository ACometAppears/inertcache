Based in the outskirts of London, InertProductions offer services in the area of recording, mixing, mastering and post-production. With 6 years of experience in the field of recording, audio engineering, and in depth knowledge utilising a range of high-end inline & Digital consoles, Analogue gear, and all major DAW's (Cubase/Nuendo, Samplitude, ProTools, Logic) the results are always to your expectation regardless of the tools used.

Aiming to provide the highest degree in terms of quality with a unique attention to detail, we do more than just press 'Record'.

We are always happy to discuss any project you may have in mind, so feel free to contact us with any questions you may have with regards to scheduling, logistics or a quote.
